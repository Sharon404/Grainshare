Who we are

Founded in 2020, Grainshare Limited is an agritech company focused on improving the crop supply chain. We combine local agricultural knowledge with practical digital tools to increase farmer incomes and reduce post-harvest losses.

Our approach

We design simple, reliable systems that make it easier to track produce, coordinate logistics, and discover fair market prices - all with minimal disruption to existing farmer practices.

Credibility

Officially registered and operating across our region - we prioritise transparency, data integrity and strong partnerships with local cooperatives and traders.
