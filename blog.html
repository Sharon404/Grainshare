Meeting with Small-Scale Farmers in Siaya County

Published on November 18, 2025

We recently had the privilege of meeting with small-scale farmers across Siaya County to understand their challenges and gather feedback on our services. The conversations were inspiring and reinforced our commitment to supporting small-scale farmers in their journey toward fair markets and sustainable growth.

Farmers shared their experiences with traditional supply chains, the uncertainties they face, and their hopes for a more transparent and equitable system. These insights directly inform our product development and service improvements.

Farm Visit: A Day in the Field

Published on November 10, 2025

Our team visited farms across the region to assess post-harvest practices and understand the logistical challenges that small-scale farmers face. We observed firsthand how proper handling and storage can significantly impact crop quality and market value.

These insights are helping us develop better training programs and aggregation strategies to reduce post-harvest losses and ensure small-scale farmers get fair prices for their produce.
