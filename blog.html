Meeting with Small-Scale Farmers in Siaya County

Published on November 18, 2025

We recently had the privilege of meeting with small-scale farmers across Siaya County to understand their challenges and gather feedback on our services. The conversations were inspiring and reinforced our commitment to supporting small-scale farmers in their journey toward fair markets and sustainable growth.

Farmers shared their experiences with traditional supply chains, the uncertainties they face, and their hopes for a more transparent and equitable system. These insights directly inform our product development and service improvements.

Farm Visit: A Day in the Field

Published on November 10, 2025

Our team visited farms across the region to assess post-harvest practices and understand the logistical challenges that small-scale farmers face. We observed firsthand how proper handling and storage can significantly impact crop quality and market value.

These insights are helping us develop better training programs and aggregation strategies to reduce post-harvest losses and ensure small-scale farmers get fair prices for their produce.

Brush Cutter & Tiller Demonstration for Small-Scale Farmers

Published on December 2, 2025

We recently hosted a hands-on demonstration of brush cutters and tillers for small-scale farmers in Siaya County. These mechanized tools are transforming how farmers prepare their fields and manage crop residue, significantly reducing labor time and costs while improving soil health for better harvests.

Watch our video demonstration below to see these tools in action:

Key Benefits for Small-Scale Farmers:

  • Time Savings: Brush cutters clear fields in hours instead of days, allowing farmers to focus on other critical tasks
  • Cost Reduction: Less labor-intensive than manual clearing, reducing seasonal labor costs significantly
  • Better Soil Preparation: Tillers create uniform soil conditions, improving seed germination and crop establishment
  • Sustainable Farming: Proper soil preparation reduces reliance on excessive fertilizers and improves long-term soil health
  • Market Competitiveness: Better-prepared fields lead to healthier crops and improved yields for better market prices

Through our partnership network, Grainshare can help small-scale farmers access these tools through equipment-sharing models, making mechanization affordable and accessible. If you're interested in learning more about brush cutter and tiller services for your farm, contact us today.
