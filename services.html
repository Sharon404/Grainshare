Our Services

Building fair and transparent agricultural supply chains is our mission - every farmer's effort deserves value and every grain carries a family's hope.

Crop Supply Chain

Our core service connects smallholder farmers directly to reliable buyers through a system that values fairness, transparency, and trust. From farm to market, Grainshare simplifies how crops move through the supply chain, ensuring that farmers get the right prices and buyers get verified quality.

Key features

  • Field-level data capture and record keeping
  • Quality assessment and standardised grading
  • Aggregation points and cold storage coordination
  • Transparent pricing and buyer matchmaking
  • Basic training for post-harvest handling and digital literacy

Benefits

  • Fairer income for farmers through better price discovery - more predictable cash for families.
  • Reduced post-harvest losses with streamlined logistics - protecting both food and livelihoods.
  • Simpler coordination between farms, aggregators, and markets - saving time and stress for farmers and buyers.
  • Full traceability that builds trust across the value chain - buyers can trace the story behind every sack.

This is only the beginning. As Grainshare grows, we will introduce new tools to strengthen the agricultural value chain - from farm management insights to sustainable trading platforms. We're committed to making farming more rewarding for everyone.
