Our Services

Focused on the Crop Supply Chain — efficient, transparent and farmer-centered solutions.

Crop Supply Chain

Our core service connects growers to buyers through transparent aggregation, quality checks, and logistics coordination. We help reduce intermediaries and ensure fairer prices for farmers.

Key features

  • Field-level data capture and record keeping
  • Quality assessment and standardised grading
  • Aggregation points and cold storage coordination
  • Transparent pricing and buyer matchmaking
  • Basic training for post-harvest handling

Benefits

  • Improved farmer incomes through better price discovery
  • Lower post-harvest losses
  • Simplified logistics and reduced delays
  • Documented records that enable traceability and trust
