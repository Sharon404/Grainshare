We Are Grainshare

Born from the soil, built for small-scale farmers. We use technology to bring fairness, connection, and transparency to the agricultural supply chain for smallholder communities.

Our Story

Grainshare began with a simple truth; that the heartbeat of our food system lies in the hands of small-scale farmers. For too long, their hard work has gone unseen, their produce undervalued. We built Grainshare to change that, to give small-scale farmers a fair, transparent, and reliable way to move their produce and earn what their work truly deserves.

I'm not just a founder, I am a farmer too. I've felt the uncertainty of market days, the struggle to find fair buyers, and the frustration of good harvests losing value. Grainshare was born from those experiences - from the need to make small-scale farming more rewarding, not just for one of us, but for all farmers in our communities.

Today, we stand as a bridge between small-scale farmers and fair markets, powered by technology, guided by empathy, and driven by the belief that every farmer's seed and every harvest matters.

Mission

To give small-scale farmers back their power to grow, through tools that simplify trading, insights that drive growth, and connections that ensure fairness and sustainability at every step.

Vision

A future where every farmer's effort is recognized, every harvest brings dignity, and technology works hand in hand with people to build a stronger agricultural world.

Our Focus: Sorghum for Small-Scale Farmers

Sorghum is at the heart of our mission. This resilient crop thrives in diverse climates and requires minimal inputs, making it ideal for small-scale farmers across East Africa. Sorghum not only feeds families and communities but also provides nutritional security and income opportunities.

We have partnered with EABL (East African Breweries Limited), our leading sorghum buyer, to create reliable markets for small-scale farmers. This partnership ensures that farmers have guaranteed buyers, fair prices, and predictable income for their sorghum harvest. Together, we're building a sustainable sorghum value chain that benefits farmers, buyers, and communities.
