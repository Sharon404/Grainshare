About Grainshare

Our mission to transform agricultural supply chains

Our Story

Since 2020, Grainshare Limited has worked closely with smallholder farmers, cooperatives and market actors to create fairer and more efficient crop supply channels. Our work reduces post-harvest losses, improves price discovery and connects produce to higher-value buyers.

Mission

To enable sustainable agricultural livelihoods through accessible technology and transparent supply chain practices.

Vision

A thriving farming community where technology amplifies value for every stakeholder.

CEO message

"At Grainshare our focus is simple: to create systems that respect the work of farmers while bringing the benefits of data and connectivity to the field. We start small, listen to our partners, and scale solutions that improve incomes and build resilient supply chains."

— Sharon Aoko, CEO
